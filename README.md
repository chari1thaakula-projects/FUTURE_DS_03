📊 Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes marketing campaign performance using the Bank Marketing Dataset from the University of California Irvine.

The objective was to evaluate customer conversion behavior, identify funnel drop-offs, and generate actionable insights to improve lead-to-customer conversion rates.

The dataset contains 45,211 marketing contact records from a Portuguese banking institution's term deposit campaigns.

🎯 Business Objective

To answer key marketing questions:

Where are customers dropping off in the funnel?

Which customer segments convert the most?

Does campaign frequency impact performance?

How does previous engagement influence conversion?

Which contact strategies are most effective?

🔍 Key Insights

Overall conversion rate is approximately 11–12%.

Customers previously contacted convert significantly higher.

Longer call durations increase subscription probability.

Excessive campaign follow-ups reduce effectiveness.

Specific customer segments (management, retired) show higher conversion performance.

💡 Business Recommendations

Prioritize customers with previous successful engagement.

Optimize campaign frequency to avoid customer fatigue.

Increase focus on high-value customer segments.

Improve targeting strategy using financial and demographic segmentation.
